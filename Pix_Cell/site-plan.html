Why will people come to your site? *To get help finding reliable solutions to their devices problems.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them? *What services does the company provide? Do they have the skills and resources that it is needed to do the work? Can I trust on their services?

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? *Know what to expect from the company, who is their professional and how to contact the company.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers? *Our contacts, our services information and professional skills of the team.
